About our project

In modern times,people in their busy schedule,unable to remember their works what they need to perform overall the day.Especially as teenagers keep growing day by day memory power may be decreased due to stress so that they cannot remember multiple tasks at a time. Imagine an old man have to take medicines at a particular time,Due to his busy schedule or low memory power in that age he had forgot to take those medicines and now his health will be in danger.Lets say a lady is passionate in growing plants and in order to save them she fill them daily.Suddenly once she forgot to pour water due to her busy schedule and plants get dry.Now there will be chance of risk to plants life.In order to address these types of issues we have come up with an app "Bring TO MIND".In this app we can make the note of list if all works to be done,When the time comes the alarm will be ring.So this make us to remember our work and can do the work without fail.This app is more useful for old aged people and busy people.This app makes them to perform all of their activities without fail.

REQUIREMENTS:

  1. Android Studio Code
  2. Laptop
  3. Kotlin Language
WORKING PRINCIPLE
1. INSTALL THE ANDROID STUDIO: If you haven't,download and install the Android Studio from the official Android Studio website
2. UPLOAD THE CODE:
Open the Android Studio
Write the code in Kotlin according to the app requirements
Upload the code in Android Studio
3. When we open the app we can see the task bar and time adjustment bar.There we need to place(or)write the works we need to do over a particular time and set the time
4. When the time in our phone reaches to the time we set in the app we receive an alarm type notification which makes u to remember the work so that we complete work without fail.

SUMMARY:
The outcome is that the code written in the app make to ring the alarm.When the time reaches to time set by the user,makes them to remember their work and they can do it without fail.We can further develop it with higher advancements like AI We can also customise this project by:
Adjusting the time(or)changing its placement to better suits you need
Modifying the alarm duration and sound pattern by altering the delay and control in code
Adding additional features such as writing the to do list
